About Me

Hey there! I'm Bukarr, and I'm thrilled to share my web development journey. I use this platform to document my adventures in the Front-end Web Development and I'm passionate about coding and creating interactive and engaging web experiences.
I'm a final year student of the federal university of education, Zaria. Where i studied computer/integrated science. I started my tech journey years back when i was in secondary school and i fully dived into it when i went for my SIWES in 2023 at NCAT (Nigeria college of aviation technology, Zaria).
I’m a web designer and developer based in Zaria, Kaduna, state Nigeria. I create clean, responsive websites and digital experiences that help people connect, explore, and grow. I care about thoughtful design, smooth performance, and making things feel intuitive—whether it’s a brand-new idea or a fresh redesign. I work with tools like HTML/CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap to bring ideas to life. I love solving problems, learning new things, and turning creative sparks into real, usable products. When I’m not building online experiences, you might find me sketching ideas, exploring new tech, or diving into a good playlist. Let’s build something people love to use.
